Achievements

Team Fusion works hard at what it does and never does that work go unreognized. Our work has earned us a vast array of awards and recognition. This includes President Barack Obama and many more.

Recognition

President Barack Obama - In 2015, President Obama sent Team Fusion #364 a letter of approval and encouragement

Mississippi Governer Ronnie Musgrove issued Team Fusion the State of Mississippi Commitment to Excellence Award

Former Gulfport Mayor Bob Short declared February 27 "Team Fusion Day"

The Sun Herald, our local newspaper, and WLOX TV, our local news station, have featured Team Fusion in numerous articles
