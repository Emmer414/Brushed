First Stronghold

Acquire knowledge about this year's 2016 FRC game, FIRST Stronghold

FIRST Stronghold is the 2016 FIRST Robotics Competition game for 2016. In this game, robots are used to cross “castle walls” – different obstacles outlining the “courtyard” – and score “boulders” into the castle towers at the opposite end of the field. This year's game has a hidden meaning, heavily promoting communication and working with constraints and obstacles. The drivers must communicate with their “spy” or exclusively drive through on-board cameras. This will challenge many people to work together between their own members and with other teams. To find out more about this year’s game, click to view the game reveal video or go to firstinspires.org. May your castles be strong!
