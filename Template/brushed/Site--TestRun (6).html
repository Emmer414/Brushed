Team Fusion

About Mississippi's rising robotics team

Team Fusion #364 is an FRC team founded in 1999. Since its conception, Team Fusion has been a driving force in FIRST robotics competitions and continues today. To learn more, click on the picture above..

First Stronghold

Acquire knowledge about this year's FRC game, FIRST Stronghold

FIRST Stronghold is the 2016 FIRST Robotics Competition game for 2016. In this game, robots are used to cross “castle walls” – different obstacles outlining the “courtyard” – and score “boulders” into the castle towers at the opposite end of the field. This year's game has a hidden meaning, heavily promoting communication and working with constraints and obstacles. The drivers must communicate with their “spy” or exclusively drive through on-board cameras. This will challenge many people to work together between their own members and with other teams. May your castles be strong!
